Penning down notes, creating works of art, and learning more about the world they live in — this was made possible thanks to our partnership with Pilot.

Last December, WeeTrip gave lessons to the children of Ban Houehing village. From learning basic introductions in English to ASEAN country flags, the children showed an earnest desire for new knowledge.

Pilot’s wide range of stationery meant that our lessons were brought to life, as the children played with colours and textures. By putting things down on paper, the children were better able to commit each word and lesson to memory. At the end of the trip, we gifted these stationeries to the school.

Thank you to our sponsor Pilot for coming on board again and leaving a mark in the lives of these children! ∎
