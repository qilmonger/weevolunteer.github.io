WeeTrip

Across Southeast Asia, there are many communities still in poverty and we should not turn a blind eye to our neighbours who are in need.

WeeTrip is an annual overseas service project organised by WeeVolunteer for students in WKWSCI. Over the years, teams have various countries in Southeast Asia, including Laos and Vietnam.

We aim to offer different yet sustainable volunteer opportunities with a media focus, by embarking on long-term projects that can make use of our expertise as communications students.

Luang Prabang City and
Ban Houehing Village, Oudomxay Province, Laos
5 — 19 December 2019

The team spent a week in Ban Houehing Village building a relationship with the welcoming villagers and assisted with assembling a water filtration system for the village. They also returned to the village WeeTrip visited last year, Ban Houehin, to repair the water pipe that was damaged by bad weather. The spent the next seven days in the city centre making use of their communication skills by collecting stories and capturing images to spread awareness about lesser-known stories and issues facing our neighbours in Laos.
