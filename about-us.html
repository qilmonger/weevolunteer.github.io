About Us

Who We Are

The WKWSCI Volunteer Programme, or WeeVolunteer, is a student-led organisation that aims to benefit both local and overseas communities in need.

It is run by undergraduates from the Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information (WKWSCI) at the Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

WeeVolunteer is an ad hoc body formed under the Communication and Information Club, the official student club that represents and serves the student body of WKWSCI.

The organisation is headed by an Executive Committee â€“ responsible for directing and spearheading new initiatives while the 15-member Main Committee plans, executes and oversees the volunteering activities.

Vision

To serve as a beacon for
volunteer organisations in NTU.


Mission

To inspire a cohort of WeeKids to give back to society, by providing different, sustainable volunteer opportunities with a communication focus.
